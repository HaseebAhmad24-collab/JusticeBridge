# Tax Laws of Pakistan

## Income Tax Ordinance 2001
The Income Tax Ordinance 2001 is the primary legislation governing the levy and collection of income tax in Pakistan. It covers:
- **Taxable Income:** Income from salary, property, business, capital gains, and other sources.
- **Tax Rates:** Progressively tiered rates for individuals, AOPs, and companies.
- **Withholding Tax:** Mechanisms for tax deduction at source for various transactions.
- **Federal Board of Revenue (FBR):** The apex tax authority responsible for administration.

## Sales Tax Act 1990
The Sales Tax Act 1990 governs the value-added tax system on the supply and import of goods.
- **Standard Rate:** Usually 18% (subject to budget changes).
- **Registration:** Mandatory for manufacturers, wholesalers, and major retailers.
- **Input Tax Credit:** Ability for registered persons to adjust tax paid on purchases against tax collected on sales.

## Federal Excise Act 2005
Levied on specific goods and services produced in or imported into Pakistan, such as beverages, cigarettes, and certain financial services.
