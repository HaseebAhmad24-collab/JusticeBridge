# Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016

## Core Offenses
The PECA 2016 is the primary law dealing with cybercrimes in Pakistan. Key sections include:

### Section 3: Unauthorized Access to Information System
Unauthorized access to any information system or data is a punishable offense.

### Section 10: Cyber Terrorism
Accessing a computer system to cause fear, panic, or insecurity, or to advance a religious/sectarian cause through violence.

### Section 11: Hate Speech
Disseminating electronic messages that incite hatred against any group based on religion, sect, or ethnicity.

### Section 20: Offenses Against Dignity of a Natural Person (Defamation)
Sending or displaying false information which intimidates or harms the reputation of a person.

### Section 21: Cyber Stalking
Harassing or intimidating a person through electronic communication.

## Investigation Agency
The **Federal Investigation Agency (FIA)** and its Cyber Crime Wing are responsible for enforcing this act.
