# Labor Laws of Pakistan

## Factories Act 1934
The Factories Act governs the working conditions of laborers in factories. Key provisions include:
- **Working Hours:** Maximum 48 hours per week and 9 hours per day.
- **Health and Safety:** Requirements for cleanliness, ventilation, lighting, and protection against fire and dangerous machinery.
- **Holidays:** Mandatory weekly holidays and annual leaves with pay.

## Payment of Wages Act 1936
Ensures that wages are paid to employees in a timely manner and without unauthorized deductions.
- **Payment Cycle:** Wages must be paid within 7 to 10 days after the end of the wage period.
- **Deductions:** Only specific deductions (e.g., for absence from duty, damage to goods) are permitted.

## Industrial Relations Act (IRA) 2012
Regulates the formation of trade unions and the settlement of industrial disputes.
- **Right to Organize:** Workers have the right to form and join trade unions.
- **Collective Bargaining:** Unions can negotiate with employers on behalf of workers.
- **NIRC:** The National Industrial Relations Commission handles major disputes and union registrations at the federal level.
